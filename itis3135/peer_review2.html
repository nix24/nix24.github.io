Project Peer Review 2

This page will be dedicated in giving a in-depth review on a Website created for a client. This website was created by Chris Hanson

Home Page:

At first glance, the color scheme is a nice red/black combination. The black helps to balance out the intensity of lighter shade of red. Overall it's a solid color scheme. The only critisism I have deals with the theme of the website.The website revolves around glass blowing. Since this is the home page, It's a little confusing that there isn't anything related to it when a user first opens the page. I would include a related image somewhere to keep the theme consistent or even a small sentence detailing what to expect.

Besides that small thing I noticed, this is still a nice home page. the navbar is nice and sleak, and the button that links to the about page is a nice eye-catcher!

About Page:

Looking at the about page, It has everything you would need for one. A relating image, and a nice concise summary about the service. Because of that, I don't have much negative things to say for the page.

Contact Page:

The contact page follows a similar format to the home page. Text is all centered at the middle with everything you need to know. This isn't wrong in the slightest. Although I feel a nitpick could be said about the lack of anything going on the page. I think more styling could go a long way for this page. Nothing complex. Instead of adding everything to the middle, Maybe have the heading formatted at the top with email and phone in a border. The way it looks ow is a little compact. Besides hat, this page has everything it needs info wise and makes for a solid contact page.
