Who am I?

I have always been someone that always dreamed of creating designs that make people want to just look in awe or feel a sense of wonder. There is so much to say that words cannot express them all. What content to expect

As of now, My art is mostly on a digital medium. I specialize in creating a sense of scale of tiny characters facing off against gigantic monsters. My style tends to utilize this to give a unique feeling seeing something cute against something so grotesque.
